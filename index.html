TBI & Recovery

Eight concussions. A lifetime of learning.

Living with a traumatic brain injury is complex, invisible, and often misunderstood. Over the years I've built up a knowledge base, partly as a reference when my own memory fails, partly because others might find it useful too.

Brain Recovery Map is a dedicated platform with a concussion reflection tool and a health and wellbeing library built from lived experience.

SHSA Advocacy

Speaking up so others don't have to stay silent

After publicly sharing my experience of workplace sexual harassment and assault, I am determined to make it easier for others to speak up, and for organisations to respond with best practice processes that actually protect people.

Shared Story Collective is for two audiences: people navigating their own experience who need support, information and community, and organisations who want to build genuinely safe, responsive workplaces. Because silence protects the wrong people.

Shared Story Collective

Resources, legal information, and a shared impact wall for people navigating workplace sexual harassment and assault. Plus best practice guidance for organisations committed to doing better.
